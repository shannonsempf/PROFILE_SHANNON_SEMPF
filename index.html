ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Shannon — a UX/UI designer based in South East Queensland. I’m passionate about crafting digital experiences that are not only visually engaging but also highly functional and user-centric. Whether it's improving user journeys, enhancing accessibility, or refining visual design, I strive to create solutions that are both practical and impactful.

CASE STUDIES

THE CAT PROTECTION SOCIETY
A redesign of the webpage for the non-for profit organisation ‘The Cat Protection Society of NSW’. Our aim: to create a website that encourages cat adoption, and enables the adoption proccess to be completed online.

DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE
Although they are an important source of information, government websites are frequently littered with design flaws that can make them difficult to use or navigate. This project used the user centred design process in order to recreate a more intuitive and user friendly webpage.

ANIMAL REHOMING SERVICE
The goal: modernise the site and improve the user experience, with a strong focus on encouraging cat adoption. The new design streamlines the adoption process, allowing users to browse available cats, learn more about each one, and complete the entire adoption journey online with ease.
